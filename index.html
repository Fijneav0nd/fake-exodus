How to Add Fake Money

Follow these simple steps to customize your wallet balance

1
⌨ Ctrl + A
🖥

Open Editor

Press Ctrl + A to open the balance editor panel.

2
$ Amount
💲

Enter Balance

Enter the amount you want to display in your wallet.

3
💾 Save
📋

Save Changes

Click Save to apply your new balance amount.

4
⌨ Ctrl + A
✅

Confirm

Press Ctrl + A again to confirm and close the editor.
